Harbin the Giant-tamer
Level 4 Cleric

His story

Harbin is an itinerant priest of the Lady of Silence, goddess of life and death, and an impassioned hunter of the undead and necromancers. He's recently joined a group of adventurers who have come to be known as "The Headhunters."

Ongoing Quests
